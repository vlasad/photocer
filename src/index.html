How to become photographer?

Before or even after making the leap into full-time photography, learn everything you need to know about excelling as a pro.

The ambition is to turn your pastime of photography into a full-time paid dream job as a professional photographer. While some people do live that ideal, it's not as simple as Instagram photographs make it appear.

Transitioning from a hobbyist to a professional photographer requires a lot of time, as well as a lot of study and hands-on experience.
